Top three aplaces to visit at Istanbul

Fener and Balat neighborhoods

These suburbs were added to the UNESCO World Heritage List as part of the historical old city in Istanbul.

Aqueduct of Valens

There’s a distribution plant near Hagia Sophia. The aqueduct brought water into the city, then stored in underground cisterns.

Belgrad Forest

This forest is a protected woodland area by the Black Sea. It’s also referred to as the lungs of Istanbul.
